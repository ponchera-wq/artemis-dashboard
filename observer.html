What is Observer Mode?

The Artemis II Observer transforms NASA's deep-space telemetry into a local guide for your backyard. While the main dashboard shows where Orion is in space, this page calculates exactly where to point your telescope from your specific coordinates on Earth. Use the steps below to find, track, and photograph the spacecraft during its journey to the Moon.
